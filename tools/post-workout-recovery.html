Recovery Nutrition Science

Protein for Repair

Consume 20-40g of high-quality protein within 30-120 minutes post-workout to maximize muscle protein synthesis and repair damaged tissues.

Carbs for Replenishment

Replenish glycogen stores with 1-1.2g of carbs per kg body weight for endurance sessions, or 0.5-0.8g/kg for strength training.

The 3:1 Ratio

For most athletes, a 3:1 or 4:1 carb-to-protein ratio optimizes both glycogen replenishment and muscle protein synthesis simultaneously.

Hydration is Critical

Replace 150% of fluid lost during exercise. For every kg of body weight lost, consume 1.5L of fluid over the next 2-6 hours.
