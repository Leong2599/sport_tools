Safe Weight Management Principles

Sustainable Rates

For most athletes, 0.5-1% of body weight per week is sustainable for fat loss, while 0.25-0.5% is optimal for muscle gain with minimal fat.

Performance Preservation

Aggressive weight cuts (>2% body weight/week) significantly impair strength, power, and endurance. Moderate approaches maintain performance better.

Body Composition Focus

Prioritize body composition over scale weight. Muscle preservation during cuts and lean gains during bulks are key for long-term success.

Physiological Adaptation

The body adapts to sustained calorie deficits. Regular refeeds and diet breaks prevent metabolic slowdown and psychological burnout.
