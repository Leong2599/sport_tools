Pre-Workout Nutrition Principles

Timing is Everything

Eating 2-3 hours before exercise allows for optimal digestion and energy availability. Closer to workout time, focus on easily digestible carbs.

Carbohydrates are Key

Carbs provide the primary fuel for high-intensity exercise. Aim for 1-4g per kg body weight, depending on workout intensity and duration.

Protein Support

Include 10-20g of protein to reduce muscle breakdown, especially for strength training sessions lasting over 60 minutes.

Hydration Foundation

Start well-hydrated by drinking 500-600ml of water 2-3 hours before exercise, and another 200-300ml 20-30 minutes before starting.
