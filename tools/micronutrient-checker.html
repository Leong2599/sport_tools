Understanding Micronutrients

What are Micronutrients?

Micronutrients are vitamins and minerals required by the body in small amounts but are essential for growth, disease prevention, and overall wellbeing.

Why Athletes Need More

Increased physical activity raises micronutrient demands through sweat losses, increased metabolic turnover, and tissue repair needs.

Score Interpretation

Scores above 80% indicate adequate intake, 60-80% shows moderate adequacy, while below 60% suggests potential deficiencies.
