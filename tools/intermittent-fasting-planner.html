Intermittent Fasting & Training

Fasting Methods

16:8 is most popular for beginners, while 18:6 and 20:4 offer enhanced fat burning. Advanced methods like Eat-Stop-Eat provide periodic metabolic benefits.

Training During Fasting

Low-to-moderate intensity training works well fasted. For high-intensity sessions, consider training just before breaking your fast.

Performance Considerations

Initial adaptation may take 2-4 weeks. Monitor performance metrics and adjust timing based on your individual response and goals.

Nutrient Timing

Focus on protein intake during eating windows to preserve muscle mass. Time carbohydrates strategically around training sessions.
